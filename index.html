Tai Chi Master You are the greatest Tai Chi Master, and the world is suffering from the Yin Yang monster Defeat the monsters and save the world! Watch out for your Yin Yang power balance, or it will cause Qigong deviation and hurt you. Survive as long as possible!
Survival7
Guide
Visuals

Green capsule is you, the Tai Chi Master! Dont let monsters touch you!

Black(Yin) and White(Yang) cubes are monsters

Black(Yin) and White(Yang) spheres are energies

Bottom Left corner shows your skills and bottom right corner shows your stored energy

Control

Damage monsters by hiting arrow keys in the order displayed below their health bar.

Energy can be comsumed directly with Left Mouse Button, or stored with Right Mouse Button

Click on your stored energies to consume them or save them for skills

Consuming energies that are just spawned give you bigger balance shift than consuming stored ones

Click on skill buttons to cast them if you have enough stored energies as indicated

Mechanics

Your Yin-Yang balance will shift when killing monsters or consuming energies

If your Yin-Yang is out of balance, you will start suffering!

Failing to kill an enemy OR casting skills DOES NOT affect your balance

Skill Intro

Slow: slow the enemies for 5 sec

One hit kill: kill the monsters with 1 hit for 5 sec

Kill all: kill all the monsters currently on the screen
